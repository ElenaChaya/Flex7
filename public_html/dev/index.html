  • 1. I make up the script  based on the wishes reflected in the questionnaire.

  • 2. Come with a DJ in two hours before the event, install and configure the equipment.

  • 3. I greet guests in the Welcome-zone.

  • 4. Organize guests for the beginning of the holiday.

  • 5. I supervise the course of the celebration - toasts, jokes, competitions, quizzes, flash mobs, entertainments, dance breaks.

  • 6. I carefully monitor the mood of the audience, and give her only what she wants, regardless of the script written beforehand.
